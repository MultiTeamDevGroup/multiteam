Vanilla Expansion

In this tab you will find all the features added by multicraft that aims on "expanding the vanilla game".



Wooden Bark

The bark item is coming from logs. It just drops from the log when you strip it with your axe. Exactly 4 pieces of them, now you can use it to craft the stripped logs back to normal logs

New Paintings

Yes, finally! New paintings! They are being made by IAmGama, Rellit, StaboVexo and King_of_Creepers. Also you can place them with your regular old painting item. You can see all the 24 of them here:







Blue berries

An alternative for the Sweet berries... and its blue! Also they generates in the Roofed Forest biomes.





Terracotta Bricks

We had the colorful update in 1.12, but we didint get these... The terracotta bricks. These bricks are same as the normal ones, but they are made out of terracotta.

Brick Crafting: (this recipe gives 4 bricks)







Colored planks

Continuing on the Colorful update... by the way, notch planned to add colored planks to the game back then, so... here you go. we did it. You can craft them with any type of planks.

Coloring the planks: (this recipe gives 8 planks)

Hey, mossy cobblestone exists! And even mossy stone bricks are a thing, then why we dont have mossy version for the normal bricks? (slabs, walls and stairs coming soon)

Polar bear & Panda pelt

Polar bears an pandas... they are boring. Even if you kill them, they drop xp. Except polar bears drop fish, but still. And here comes Multicraft! Now they will drop their pelts! Also you can use that pelt to craft some normal leather, to be exact one plet gives you 2 leathers.






The Upgraded Aquatics [1.13]

"The Upgraded Aquatics" tab contains all the features that connects somehow to the 1.13 update, alias the "The Update Aquatic". This dosen't mean that the mod is being released for this version, just has some features that could modify this update's content, or expand on it.
