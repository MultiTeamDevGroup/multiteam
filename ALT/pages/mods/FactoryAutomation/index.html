Welcome to the official page of FactoryAutomation!


This mod overhauls the entire game basically, by creating a new chain of progression and generally making the game progress harder. Extends the Early-Game, by replacing wooden tools with flint, making mining ores a bit different, and earning usable ingots harder.

Available for Forge 1.12.2 & 1.15.2 & 1.16.5 Download
Using the old versions is not recomended. The mod is still in Alpha state, so keep that in mind.
